Protection Plan

UOwn now offers a protection plan that protects customers’ leases in the event of a covered loss or illnesses.

In addition to this, the protection plan offers many other benefits.

Protection Plan

Before offering the plan:

  • The account must be in an active leasing status
  • Customer must reside in a supported state

Do NOT offer the plan if:

  • The customer is in a non-supported state
  • The customer is calling to pay off/close their lease
  • The lease is pending closure or not eligible due to other criteria

Protection Plan

When introducing the plan, keep the tone FRIENDLY AND INFORMATIVE. We don’t want to push the customer for a sale.

Before ending the call:

“Before we wrap up, I just wanted to let you know that we now offer a protection plan that can help cover your lease in case of unexpected events like job loss or illness. It’s completely optional and you can sign up through your online portal.”

While working on an account:

“While I work on this for you, I just wanted to let you know that we now offer a protection plan that can help cover your lease in case of unexpected events like job loss or illness. It’s completely optional and you can sign up through your online portal.”

Protection Plan: Cancellation

When a customer is requesting for a cancellation on their Protection Plan:

Send the customer’s account information to your immediate supervisor to process the cancellation of the protection plan. Properly document the account regarding the customer request.

Note: Any BILLING OR REFUND inquiries regarding the protection plan must be routed to AON’s member services at 866-881-2411.
