Interpreter Calls

Interpreter calls occur when a customer requires a communication assistance due to a language barrier. In these cases, a professional third-party interpreter is used to facilitate the conversation between the agent and the customer.

When an agent receives this type of call scenario, you need to ask for the interpreter’s:

  • Name
  • Interpreter’s ID Number
  • Interpretation Company

You must include this information in your notes.

Once you have confirmed the interpreter’s information, treat them as the customer. However, longer pauses are common on these types of calls. Also, you need to speak clearly and slower as they are required to interpret everything that you say.

Advise the customers utilizing interpreters to visit the customer portal as it may be a faster way to make payments and get information on the account.
