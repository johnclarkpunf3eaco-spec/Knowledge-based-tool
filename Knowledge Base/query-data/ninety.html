90-Day Promotion

The 90-Day promotion is an offer provided to customers within a 90-day time frame. It begins the moment the merchandise is tagged as delivered.

What voids the promotion:

  • Missed, returned, or late payments
  • If the promotion is not paid on or before the expiration date

Exceptions:

  • Q-Errors
  • UOwn is closed on the expiration date
  • If a payment is made before it returns

Changes Without Voiding the Promotion

  • One Due Date change
  • One Frequency change
  • Changes made before the first payment is made

Due Date changes before the first payment can only be moved 10 days out.

Inclusions

  • Cash Price
  • Sales Tax
  • Processing Fee and/or Delivery Fee (if applicable)

For the 90-day Quick Pay amount, it includes:

  • Cash Price
  • 5% or 10% of the cash price
  • Sales Tax
  • Processing Fee and/or Delivery Fee (if applicable)

Extra Payments

Customers will need to make extra payments to honor the promotion. When processing an extra payment during the 90-day, allocate the funds to the EPO Pool.

90-Day Settlement

Offered to customers who either had missed payments within the promotion or missed the payment within the 7-day grace period.

Note: 90-Day Approval without a fee can only be approved and documented by a Team Lead in the notes.

For customers who reside within California, the reinstatement fee will only be charged when they exceeded the expiration date and are still within the 7-day grace period.
