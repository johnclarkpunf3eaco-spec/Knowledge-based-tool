Lease Modifications

If a customer asked if their contract was adjusted because of a partial return/change of merchandise etc., please check the steps if adjustment was made:

  • Lease modification will indicate that changes were made to the lease.
  • A new lease will be generated in the documents tab and Type will show as LEASEMOD.
  • There will be a red alert on the account.
  • Usually, a key indicator will be a change in payment amounts.
