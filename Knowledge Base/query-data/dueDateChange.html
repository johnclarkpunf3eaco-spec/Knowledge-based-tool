Due Date Change

Located in due amounts, click the ‘Move Due Date’ button and a pop-up window will appear where you can change or move the due date.

The Scheduled Due Date field should be the due date you would want to move from, and Move Due Date field should be the desired due date we are moving to.

After making the necessary changes click SAVE to update the changes.

Due dates are not to be used to skip a payment

Any biweekly, semi-monthly, and monthly may be moved a max of 7 days – Weekly can be moved up to 3 days.
