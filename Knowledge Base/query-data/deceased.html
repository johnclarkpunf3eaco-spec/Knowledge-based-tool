Deceased

If the person who contacted us is managing the affairs of the deceased customer, but the leased item is not in their possession...

  • Escalate the account to your supervisor for proper review and handling.
  • Suggested Spiel: "Thank you for the information. Since the item is not in your possession, I will escalate this to our management for review. They will provide the next steps once a decision is made."
  • Leave a complete notation on the account afterwards.
