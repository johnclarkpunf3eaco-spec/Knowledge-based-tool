To make a payment and/or Set up an SPA…

Click the $ located at the upper right corner of the servicing system.

When processing a payment and setting up an SPA, you should…

  • Make sure that you entered the right amount, allocation type, date, and chose the right mode of payment (ACH or CC). Do not use the CHECK option.
  • Allocation Types:
    • Payment – will go through the regular payment
    • Payment/EPO – any additional amount exceeding the regular payment will go towards the EPO Balance
    • EPO only – will go towards the EPO Balance/Pool
  • When asking for the authorization, you MUST provide the MAD (Mode of Payment, Amount, and Date).
NOTE: Do not choose the allocate payment towards EPO unless the account is current or within the 90 days – anything past that needs to be allocated to regular payments ONLY.

Please add/update the Rating Letter to "P Rating" on the Servicing Information Section to stop Autopay and cease collection efforts temporarily. If the SPA declines, the rating will be automatically removed and turned on.

If the customer calls in to pay their scheduled payment in advance…

  • Process the payment
  • Delete the scheduled payment
  • Remove the P Rating
