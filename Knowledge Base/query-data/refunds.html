Refunds

A refund will be given if:

  • Duplicate Payments or Customer overpaid due to UOwn's error
  • Payment was processed through the wrong payment method or wrong account.
  • Customer paid off/settled, but autopay continued to process
  • Customer had an active payment arrangement or is already in the MR process and a payment was processed.

Refund Process

The agent can submit a refund request to their team lead who will be filling out an official refund request form and sending it to refunds@uownleasing.com. All refunds must be reviewed and approved, and should not be guaranteed until this step is processed.

If the payment was processed through a checking or savings account within the past three (3) business days, the customer can submit a bank statement to expedite the process. Ask the customer to send a proof of payment clearing, with the beginning and ending balance, to docs@uownleasing.com.
