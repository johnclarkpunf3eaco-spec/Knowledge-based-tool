UOWN LEASING

NOT GETTING MESSAGES

What if the customer isn’t getting the text?

  • Verify if the phone number is correct.
  • Verify if the customer is in an area with good cellular service.
  • Some stores in shopping centers may not have good cellular service,
  • Attempt to send to another phone number. The customer must be the individual to fill out the application.

NOT GETTING EMAIL

What if the customer isn’t getting the email?

  • Verify the email is correct.
  • Ask customer to check their Spam/Junk folder.
  • If found there, customer needs to remove it from those folders.
  • Recommend the customer mark the email as “Not Spam” so future emails come through normally.

ERROR SUBMITTING THE APPLICATION

Error: What if there’s an error when submitting the application?

  • Verify the customer has good cellular reception (Error = “Something went wrong”).
  • Note what the error says, and at which step in the application process the error had occurred.
  • Ask the customer for a screenshot of the error.

ERROR SCANNING THEIR ID

What if the customer cannot scan their ID?

  • Scan the back first then the front.
  • Minimize any glare on the picture.
  • Verify the customer’s cellular service is strong.
  • If it still doesn’t scan, attempt from another device.
  • Verify the name on the ID matches the name on the application (full name without any suffix).

CANNOT SIGN THE LEASE

What if the customer can’t sign the lease?

  • Disable pop up blockers that might be enabled on the device.

STATUS NOT CHANGING

What if the customer’s status is not changing to “Signed” after they sign the lease?

  • Verify the customer selected “Complete and Sign” at the bottom of the document.
  • Verify if a customer received an email confirmation.
  • If the customer has a copy of the signed lease in their email, please ask them to forward us the document.

CAN’T LOG IN TO THE PORTAL

Error: Customer couldn’t log into their portal

  • Verify the customer has an active account.
  • Inactive accounts won’t be able to access the portal.
  • Ask the customer what internet browser they are using. Recommend using Chrome.
  • If they are still unable to log in, recommend clearing their browser’s cache.
