Rerun

Definition: Reprocessing of a payment due to a previous declined transaction.

Details

Most customers are set up on auto pay using ACH and CC. If they choose to mail a check or money order, they will be responsible for any fees and potential loss of the 90-Day Promotional Offer.

Auto pay declined payments may be reprocessed in an attempt to make up for that missed payment. If the payment clears during this process, they may be subject to an NSF Fee of $15.
