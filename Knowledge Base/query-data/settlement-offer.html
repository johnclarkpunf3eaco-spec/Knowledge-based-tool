When you can offer a settlement

You can offer a settlement when:

  • They are outside of their contract
  • Customer is seeking recovery (pick up) of items
  • Hardship

Guidelines

  • 91-150 DPD: 50% of full remaining contract balance within 30 days
  • 150+ DPD: 75% of full remaining contract balance within 30 days

Supervisor Approval

Anything outside the guidelines needs to be approved by the Supervisor.
