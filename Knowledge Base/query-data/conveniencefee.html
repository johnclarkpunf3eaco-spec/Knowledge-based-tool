Convenience Fee
UOWN LEASING

Overview

A Convenience Fee is charged when the customer pays using a debit or credit card.

The fee is $1 per payment transaction.

Applies to both one-time payments and autopay transactions made with a card.

When It Applies

The Convenience Fee applies to:

  • Payments made over the phone using debit/credit cards
  • Payments made via autopay using debit/credit cards
  • Online payments made using debit/credit cards

No fee is charged when using ACH or bank account payments.

Customer Notification

Always advise the customer of the $1 Convenience Fee before processing their payment.

Make sure they understand this fee is non-refundable.

Important Notes

Convenience Fees are automatically applied when a debit/credit card is selected as the payment method.

The fee amount and terms may vary by state law and policy updates.
