Fraud

If a customer calls in and claims fraudulent activities, we need to ask for the documents below to complete our investigation:

  • Local police department report (NOT an Incident Report)
  • Federal Trade Commission Report (https://www.identitytheft.gov/#)
  • Request a copy of their driver’s license or valid ID
  • Request proof of address (utility bill, cc statement)
  • Request any additional proof of fraud

Request all items above be emailed to fraud@uownleasing.com

Make sure to verify the customer's email address and phone number and include it in your notes. Don't make any change towards their verified information.

If you receive a call related to fraud, or you suspect that fraud may be taking place, even if the customer doesn’t use the word “Fraud”, this should be reported to your TL or Jeff Rupert.

If a customer reports instances of Phishing or Scams pretending to be Uown, these need to be reported to your TL or Jeff Rupert.
